Theory

The deformation of the beam is usually expressed in terms of its original deflection from its original unloaded position. The deflection of beam is measured from the original neutral surface to the neutral surface of the deformed beam. The curve showing the deflected shape with the applied load is known as the elastic curve of the beam.
